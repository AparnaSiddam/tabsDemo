About

Skill development programs aim to enhance the capabilities of individuals by providing them with the necessary skills, knowledge, and competencies to improve their employability or entrepreneurial prospects.

These programs are designed to bridge the gap between the skills available in the workforce and the skills required by employers, ultimately boosting the economic growth of a nation.
